Target Audience

Personas

Businesses

The main focus of our website will be business owners who wish to affiliate to our Chamber of Commerce or wish to know more about the benefits we offer.

Consumers

We also wish to make something like an online marketplace for people who are interested in local businesses to have an online directory of affiliated businesses or service providers they can rely on.

Scenarios:

An entrepreneur wishes to join: Someone wishes to start a new business in the area, but would like to know how is the competence, how viable his business model would be and how the community is.

A potential client is looking for services Someone wants to find a local business or shop that offers a service or good he requires. He can check our directory to find what he needs.
